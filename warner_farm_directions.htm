Directions to Warner Farm

Jonathon Warner's farm is about 25 minutes south of Frederick on Rt-85/Rt-28 in Dickerson, MD. It's always a good idea to MapQuest or Google map new places for directions, the actual street address is: - 20500 Martinsburg Road, Dickerson, MD 20842

Google Directions
Text Directions:

If you are coming from Germantown you can use Rt-28 and if you are close to Leesburg, VA you can take Whites ferry. I'll give directions from these places below.

If coming from the north (Frederick or Pennsylvania):
  • From Frederick, take Rt-15/270 South to Rt-85/Buckeystown Pike South (10 miles). Rt-85 south will become Rt-28 south (5 miles).


  • Enter Montgomery County and pass through the small town of Dickerson.


  • About a mile past the town Rt-28 makes a 90-degree left turn (flashing orange light).


  • Bear right to turn onto Martinsburg Road.


  • Note: The sign for Martinsburg Road is very small – you probably won't even see it so slow down as you approach the flashing orange light


  • On Martinsburg Road you will go down a hill then up another and bear left to avoid entering an industrial center. The road becomes a one lane concrete road, at the fork bear right - follow signs for warm water fishing.


  • Pass Dickerson Conservation Park on the right go up the hill and the farm is on the right – there should be some corner flags beside the gate.


  • The pitch is behind the house so go past the house and turn at the gate with the rugby ball on the pos, drive to the left of a mall barn/shed and park in the field.
If coming from the south (Northern Virginia or Germantown/Rockville):
  • Take I-95 north to I-495 (beltway) towards Tyson's corner/Rockville.


  • Then I-495 to I-270 north towards Rockville/Frederick You need to get in the local lanes.


  • Take Rt-28 West (away from Rockville). On Rt-28 you will travel out of the city and into the country a few miles from I-270. As you approach Dickerson, Rt-28 makes a 90-degree right turn (flashing orange light).


  • Bear left to turn onto Martinsburg Road. The sign for Martinsburg Road is very small – you probably won't even see it so slow down as you approach the flashing orange light. On Martinsburg Road you will go down a hill then up another and bear left to avoid entering an industrial center. The road becomes a one lane concrete road, at the fork bear right - follow signs for warm water fishing. Pass Dickerson Conservation Park on the right go up the hill and the farm is on the right – there should be some corner flags beside the gate.


  • The pitch is behind the house so go past the house and turn at the gate with the rugby ball on the pos, drive to the left of a mall barn/shed and park in the field.
If coming from Leesburg, VA:
  • The pitch is behind the house so go past the house and turn at the gate with the rugby ball on the pos, drive to the left of a mall barn/shed and park in the field.


  • From Leesburg, take Rt-15 North to Rt-655/Whites Ferry Road (1.3 miles).


  • Turn right onto Whites Ferry Road and follow to the ferry (1.2 miles).


  • Turn left off the ferry to stay on Whites Ferry Road (2.7 miles).


  • Take your first left onto Martinsburg Road and follow to farm on your left (2.4 miles). There will be some touch flags and a few cars on the road outside the farm. If you pass Dickerson Conservation Park on your left you have gone too far so just turn around. The pitch is behind the house; turn at the gate with the rugby ball on the post, follow the track beside a small barn/shed and park on the field.
