2014 Touch Tournament

Mobile-Friendly Schedule | Wild Geese Google Calendar

Saturday 14 June 2014 the Wild Geese "Old Boys" Rugby Football Club host their Second Annual touch rugby tournament at their home pitch Gosling Park (Dickerson, MD).

The tournament is open to all comers, young and old, male and female. The tournament will be divided into 2 groups: Experienced and social. We will group the Low drag, high speed teams into one group and the social teams into separate group. Teams may switch groups. Matches will be 2 x 12 minute halves with a 5 minute rest period. Rolling subs will be used.

Captains Meeting 10:30 Sharp! Tournament Start 11:00 AM

All matches will be played under FIT Rules.
Entry Fees: $200.00 per team @ 12 persons; $20 per head additional. Under 12 yrs old free.

Proceeds will go to Hope In South Africa Charity and Wild Geese Charities.

A meal will be provided as well as some libations for adults. Water will be available to all teams. Please bring your own shade tent and folding chairs, snack and beverages. (age appropriate drink laws apply)

Gosling Park is a beautiful historical private farm with a rugby pitch which will be divided into 2 fields. Located near Poolesville MD on the C&O Canal. Be sure and bring the family and kids. The tournament is a fun day out with friendly but competitive touch.

RSVP: Barr Snyderwine email: Barr@Snyderwine.com

Gosling Park is located near Dickerson Conservation Park between MD 107 and MD28, on the Potomac River near Whites' Ferry. The closest Interstate is I-270
