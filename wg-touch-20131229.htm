Wild Geese Touch Tournament

The Wild Geese Rugby Club is hosting a Touch Tournament on Saturday 5th October 2013 at Gosling Park, Dickerson MD 20842.

The tournament is open to all comers, young and old, male and female. The tournament will be divided into 2 groups, Experienced and soon to be experienced. We will group the Low drag, high speed teams into one group and the “soon to catch up“ teams into separate group. Teams may switch groups. Matches will be 2 x 12 minute halves with a 5 minute rest period. Rolling subs will be used. Captains Meeting 10:30 Sharp. Tournament Start 11:00 AM

All matches will be played under FIT Rules.

Entry Fees:

  • $20.00 per player
  • $10.00 per spectator
  • Under 12 yrs old free
Proceeds will go to Hope In South Africa Charity and Wild Geese Charities.

A meal will be provided towards the end of the day as well as limited libations for adults. Water will be available to all teams. Please bring your own shade tent and folding chairs, snack and beverages. (age appropriate drink laws apply) Gosling Park is a beautiful historical private farm with a rugby pitch which will be divided into 2 fields. Located near Poolesville MD on the C&O Canal. Be sure and bring the family and kids. The tournament is a fun day out with friendly but competitive touch.

Please RSVP to Clifford Hodson email: (mambo7@gmail.com) and advise how many players and teams you will be posting.

Upcoming Wild Geese Touch Events

We had a very active summer touch season with runs every week from the middle of June to the middle of August. We also have started entering teams in the Mid-Atlantic touch circuit. For those not familiar with the Federation of International Touch (FIT); touch rugby is a sport of its own with a very high level of competition and a very strong following. We hosted our own touch tournament this past fall and we will do so again this fall. In fact, we will try to encourage touch rugby at our matches either before or after so more folks can stay engaged and those who prefer that version of the game can participate.

In the meantime, for the next few weeks touch will be held Monday nights at 7:00 at TC Williams school.
