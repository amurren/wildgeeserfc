Over 45s: Ancient Warrior & Wild Geese 2009 CAN-AM Tournament

When: July 31 - August 2, 2009
Where: Saranac, NY
Tourney: The 36th Annual CAN - AM Rugby Tornament begins Friday (Jul 31) with Old Boys play throughout the day. Old Boys will rest Saturday (Aug 1) when over 100 teams in the Premier and Social Divisons are sure to provide spectacular rugby. The Old Boys play will wrap up on Sunday (Aug 2) with the Semi-Finals and Final.
Entry Fee: $100.00 per person (pays the $450 tourney fee, $60 Jesey, water, ice for cooler, etc.).
Lodging: Approximately $50 a night sharing one of two 10-person condos already reserved for 3 nights (Thursday - Sunday) or 4 nights (Thursday - Monday)

Directions to Condos: 48 Fir Lane and 52 Fir Lane.
Transportation: You can either fly (nearest airports are Saranac (SLK) and Albany (ALB) or drive (approx 9 hours from Baltimore). If you would like to drive or carpool, please indicate this in the notes section of the eVite.

   Note: Please plan to arrive Thursday night for the Friday games.
