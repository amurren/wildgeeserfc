Wild Geese Rugby Club

The Wild Geese Rugby Club is based in the Baltimore, MD, Washington, D.C., Alexandria, VA area. The Wild Geese have a 25 year tradition of "Old Boys" Rugby that is known for good play, a welcoming attitude, and great camaraderie. Starting in 2014 The Wild Geese are supporting a Touch Rugby squad. We invite players of any skill level to join our "Old Boys" squad, our touch squad, or both!

Saturday 14 June is our Second Annual Touch Tournament! See the details on our Catch A Goose Tournament page and look for the Evite on the mailing list.

Wild Geese "Old Boys" Rugby

The Wild Geese "Old Boys" (Veterans) Rugby Football Club carries on the Barbarian tradition of attracting rugby players from any rugby club or background that are dedicated to playing open, attacking rugby.

During the spring and fall seasons we play on our own pitch, Gosling Park, in Dickerson, MD, Western Montgomery County. The field offers a spectacular view of Virginia's Goose Creek watershed, looking out over the Potomac River.

To keep fit, we hold weekly refereed touch rugby matches throughout the summer and winter. The club offers 35-and-over players competitive play, camaraderie, and exceptional hospitality.
