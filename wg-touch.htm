Wild Geese Rugby Touch Team

The Wild Geese Touch Rugby started out as a way to keep fit by playing weekly refereed touch rugby matches throughout the summer and winter. The Wild Geese is now expanding to include year round touch for men and women 35-and-over under the FIT rules. Our touch rugby matches are open to anyone who enjoys fun and competitive play, camaraderie, and the reknown exceptional hospitality of the Wild Geese. Our home touch pitch is our excellent Gosling Park, but match locations change so we can play other Touch Rugby teams in the Maryland/DC/Virgina area.
