2013 Touch Tournament

The Wild Geese "Old Boys" Rugby Football Club is hosting a touch rugby tournament at Gosling Park (20500 Martinsburg Road, Dickerson, MD 20842) on Saturday 29 June starting at 11am - with a pre-match training from 10-10:30 for beginners. The tournament will raise funds for youth rugby in the D.C. area and the youth supported by "Hope in South Africa."

Open to all comers: male, female, young and old, new and experienced. You don't need to have a whole team, just come out and we will place you on a team! Youth 12 and above welcome (or speedy and experienced 10/11 year olds). Gosling Park is situated on a beautiful historic farm site with 2 pitches, not far from Poolesville, MD. Located near the C&O canal - which is great for hiking and biking - bring the family and kids for a day of fun.
Players should bring plenty of water and everyone should bring chairs and shade tents (there are no shade trees next to pitch).

Here are the 12 person teams who are confirmed:

  • Columbia
  • DC Galaxy
  • Washington Irish
  • Wildgeese (2) teams
  • Washington Scandals
  • DC Touch Association
More teams can easily be added or come individually and we'll get you on a team! While not essential, it is recommended that teams have a uniform or similar colored shirts.

Volunteers needed for the following:

  1. Tony Masciocchi is heading up the referee crew (FIT Rugby Rules)
  2. Food planning
  3. Game Day Dues collection
  4. Field Prep

Please email Brian Collins (brian.c1 AT me.com) or call him at (Seven Zero Three) 795 - 8169 if you have any questions or you want to volunteer for some game day activities.

We play using Australian FIT Rules. You can find several variations of the rules at Columbia Touch Rugby Rules.

We are asking a $20 per player or $200 per team donation for supporting local youth rugby here and with "Hope in South Africa". A donation of $5 for adults and teenagers who are not playing.
Your donations include a delicious cookout and beverages.
