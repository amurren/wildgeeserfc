Directions to Colmar Manor Park

Google Directions:
  • From Baltimore


  • From Washington DC


  • From Frederick, MD
Text Directions:
From the North:
  • Take exit 25 off the Beltway RT 495 to Baltimore Ave, and ride it down to Bladensburg Rd. (also known as Alternate Rt 1).


  • As you pass the Port Towns Shopping Center you will take a left on to 38th Ave (if you get to the cemetery you have passed it).


  • Make the first right up to Colmar Manor Park and go all the way to the top and field.
From the South:
  • Get into DC and ride New York Ave to Bladensburg Rd.


  • Make a left on Bladensburg Rd and ride it past South Dakota Ave to 38th Ave. (if you get to Port Towns Shopping center you have passed it)


  • Make a left and ride it past South Dakota Ave to 38th Ave. (if you get to Port Towns Shopping center you have passed it)


From the East::
  • Take Rt. 50 - which becomes New York Ave


  • Turn right on Bladensburg Rd


  • Make a left on Bladensburg Rd and ride it past South Dakota Ave to 38th Ave. (if you get to Port Towns Shopping center you have passed it)


  • Make a left and ride it past South Dakota Ave to 38th Ave. (if you get to Port Towns Shopping center you have passed it)
